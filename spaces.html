Experience

Three Spaces

Body, mind, soul — each dimension has its own room, its own invitation, its own silence.

Every space at Nirvanart is designed to meet you at a different layer of experience. You do not need to visit them in order. But each one waits for you.

01
अहं ब्रह्मास्मि · Aham Brahmasmi

Sangam

The Body · Physical Experience

We use the 5 senses — rupa, shabda, sparsha, rasa, gandha — to reveal how they can also be limitations. 4-sided projection, cymatics, and sensory play. Pause. Learn to manipulate your senses according to your will.

4-Sided ProjectionCymaticsExtrospectionMatterMemory
02
तत् त्वम् असि · Tat Tvam Asi

Sarvam

The Mind · Mental Body

Mind is the bridge where thought meets consciousness. Walkthroughs, arcade games, a live interactive visualizer, and an ever-changing puzzle room connecting the intersubjective and the knowing.

Play RoomSandboxVisualizerConsciousnessPresent
03
सत् चित् आनन्द · Sat Chit Ananda

Sattvam

The Soul · Energy Body

The human pod. An echo chamber. Total isolation of senses. The 5 elements — jal, prithvi, agni, vayu, akasha. Feel without thinking. The essence of all expressions.

Human PodEcho ChamberIntrospectionElementsEnergy
